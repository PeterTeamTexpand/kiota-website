Frequently asked

Questions, answered

Everything you need to know before you start.

What is Kiota Connect?
Kiota Connect lets you handle your money and buy everyday essentials right inside WhatsApp. You can send and receive money, shop, and earn rewards, all from a chat.
How does the membership work?
Joining Kiota is free. Signing up opens your e-shop, digital wallet, SIM options, and referral tools, and you get a starter bundle of essentials to begin with. No forms to fill in and no branch to visit.
What types of products can I buy on Kiota?
You can buy mobile plans, airtime top-ups, medical cover, and other everyday essentials straight from the community e-shop.
How do I earn rewards through referrals?
Share your e-shop link with friends, family, or neighbours. When they buy something, you earn, and the money goes straight into your wallet.
Is Kiota secure and compliant with regulations?
Yes. We follow POPIA rules for handling your data, so your money and your personal details stay safe and private.
Can I integrate Kiota with my services as a partner?
Yes. We work with trusted partners across wallet, insurance, and mobile services, so you can get up and running quickly. It works well for MVNOs, NGOs, and community leaders.
